About Us

Our story

AFRIPOD aims to transform lives, especially of the most vulnerable groups, through targeted, scalable interventions that achieve at-scale transformation of our key systems. We work with grassroots opinion shapers, community members and decision-makers to influence both policy and development through data-driven initiatives.
