Bio

Prior to Aseefa, served in the U.S. Navy where he was the Chief of Dermatology at the National Navy Medical Center, Assistant Residency Program Director at NCC and Assistant Professor of Dermatology Laser and Phototherapy at NNMC, and provided care to active duty military, retirees, congressional delegates and foreign diplomats to the U.S. Congress. Has authored articles in dermatology journals and presented at the annual meeting of the American Academy of Dermatology.
