Nairobi National Park

The national park in a city

Overview

Nairobi National Park is a unique wildlife sanctuary located 7km south of Nairobi's city centre, making it one of thev only national parks in the world bordering a capital city. Established in 1946, it covers an area of about 117 square kilometers and offers a remarkable experience for vistors looking to enjoy wildlife and nature without travelling far from the urban environment

Attractions in Nairobi National Park;

  • Wildlife
  • Vegatation
  • Kifaru oak
  • Black rhinos
  • Ivory burning monument site
  • Walking trails at hippo pools
  • Nairobi animal orphanage

Best time to visit the park Nairobi National Park is open all year round and can be visisted any time, but the dry season from June to September is generally the best time for wildlife watching, when animals can easily be seen around water sources. Wildlife watching is usually difficult during the raining season ( between March and May and October to November ). During these months, rainfall might restrict game drives and the grass tends to be very high.
