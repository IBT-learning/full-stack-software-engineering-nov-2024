Kenya

Discover the beauty and culture of Kenya

Where is Kenya?

Kenya is located in East Africa, bordered by Uganda, Tanzania, and the Indian Ocean to the south, Ethiopia to the north, South Sudan to the northwest, and Somalia to the east.

Independence

Kenya gained independence from British colonial rule on December 12, 1963. This day is celebrated annually as Jamhuri Day, marking the birth of the Republic of Kenya for more info about Kenyans independence click the link Kenyans Independence.

Kenya's Cultures

Kenya is a melting pot of diverse cultures, with over 40 different ethnic groups, each with its own unique traditions, languages, and customs.

  • Traditional music and dance
  • Vibrant clothing styles
  • Unique cuisines like Nyama Choma

Beautiful Sceneries

From the beaches of the Indian Ocean to the vast savannahs and towering mountains, Kenya is known for its breathtaking landscapes.

Wildlife

Kenya is home to an extraordinary variety of wildlife, including the "Big Five" (lion, elephant, buffalo, leopard, and rhinoceros), and is a top destination for safaris.
